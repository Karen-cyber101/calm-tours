FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance to secure your preferred dates, especially for our peak season (June-August). For last-minute bookings, please contact us directly for availability.

What is included in the tour price?

Our tours include accommodation, most meals, transportation during the tour, activities mentioned in the itinerary, and the services of our guides. Flights, travel insurance, and personal expenses are not included.

Do you offer custom itineraries?

Yes! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries tailored to your interests, travel style, and budget. Contact us with your ideas, and we'll craft your perfect journey.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a full refund for cancellations made 60+ days before departure. Between 30-60 days, we refund 50%. Unfortunately, we cannot refund cancellations made less than 30 days before departure.
